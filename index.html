About Semaphore

Semaphore is the annual technical festival organized by SAMCA. It is a platform where students showcase their talents in technology, creativity, and innovation. With a unique theme every year, it inspires participants to break barriers and go Beyond Boundaries.

Purpose

Encourage students to innovate, experiment, and excel in modern technology.

Goals

Provide opportunities for students to collaborate, compete, and create.

Theme

Semaphore 2025 embraces the theme of "Beyond Boundaries".
